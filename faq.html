FAQ's

Q. Who is a mystery shopper?

A. A mystery shopper is a person who poses as a real customer whilst assessing customer service levels provided by a company or organisation.

Q. How do I become a mystery shopper?

A. Complete our online application form by visiting www.vitric.in/shopper_register

Q. What does mystery shopping involve?

A. A mystery shopping 'assignment' can be a telephone call, email, website survey, face to face visit or home visit.

Q. What type of person can be a mystery shopper?

A. Mystery shoppers need to have a good memory, observational and writing skills. A mystery shopper needs to be committed, able to work to strict or tight deadlines, be enthusiastic and reliable.

Q. What is the minimum age to become a mystery shopper?

A. There is no minimum age but if we have an assignment for you while you are under 18 years we may need to obtain formal agreement from your parent or guardian.

Q. Do I have to have any qualifications to become a mystery shopper?

A. No formal qualifications are required, however you must be able to speak, read and write English competently, with good grammar and punctuation for report writing. You must have regular access to a PC, Internet and Email.

Q. Am I able to work from one of your offices?

A. No, all mystery shoppers work from their own home. You must have regular access to a PC, Internet and Email.
