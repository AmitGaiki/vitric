Mystery Shopping

Mystery Shopping is the most objective and unbiased way of find out the true feedback of the consumers. It helps not only in identifying conforming and non-conforming business units with specific areas of improvement, it also creates a platform for measuring effectiveness of training delivered to employees and recognize and reward best of the lot. Hospitality and Retail are some of the key industries that can benefit immensely from conducting regular mystery audits.
